📚 Recommended Readings

Empire of Care
Catherine Ceniza Choy (2003)
Duke University Press

This foundational text explores the history of Filipino nursing migration and how postcolonial systems shaped care labor. It critically challenges the idea of the “benevolent empire” and gives a deeply human account of diaspora and resistance.
